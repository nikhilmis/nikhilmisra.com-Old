My name is Nikhil Misra. I'm a multidisciplinary designer focusing on design systems, identity, editorial, interaction, and ideas. I'm always up for a new challenge, however.

Currently, I am a Product Designer at Hearst Newspapers. Previously was with the very talented people at Oak Studios. I hold an MPS in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design and a BFA in Graphic Design from SUNY Purchase College. I also participated in the Typography Summer School program in London.

You can usually find me taking photos, digging for music, or learning something new. These days I spend time learning type esign, coding, and making music.
